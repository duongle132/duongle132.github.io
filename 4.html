Assignment 2

Krug's definition of "convention"

In a website, convention is what we can easily recognize at first glance. It is what we've learned over time. Convention is what people agree upon.

Convention is easy to find in a website. First, when you look at the website and see 2 colors red and white for example. You will assume that these are the colors of the company/organization. Second, when you look at the layout, you can see common signs like buttons, search bar, search box, navigation bar, etc. These are common instructive signs that you see everywhere, therefore, you can recognize it easily.

Maybe convention is a rule that designers have to follow to make the website more clear to users. And to users, conventions are instructions showing them how to use the website efficiently.
